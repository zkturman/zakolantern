The Cursed Arcade

The year is 1999, and Darren is the owner of a dying arcade. Half of the games no longer work, the carpet is sticky from years of spilled sodas. The only lights in the dark building are the flashing lights of the arcade cabinets. Darren's waiting, alone again, until closing. But all kinds of things lurk in the dark...

The next day, police are called to investigate the arcade they had forgotten about. Inside, the carpet is newly stained with blood, but there's no body. All the arcade cabinets are overturned except for one, now standing in the middle of the space. The screen glows, reading Game Over...

You're invited to take a trip to the 90s and experience that October night at the Cursed Arcade during this fancy-dress party on 2 November, 2024.
